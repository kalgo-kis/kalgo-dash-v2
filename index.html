sum(positive net P&Ls) / sum(|negative net P&Ls|). Above 1.0 means winners outweigh losers in aggregate.

total_called / committed. What percentage of the investor's commitment was actually needed. Lower = more capital-efficient (the fleet funded itself sooner).
